Description:

"Kushi" is a 2001 Telugu romantic comedy film starring Pawan Kalyan and Bhoomika Chawla. Directed by S.J. Surya, the film follows the story of two college students, Siddhu and Madhu, who initially dislike each other but eventually fall in love. Their relationship faces various challenges, including misunderstandings and parental disapproval. The film is known for its catchy music and energetic performances by the lead actors.
