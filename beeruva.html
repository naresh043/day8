Description :

"Beeruva" is a 2014 Telugu-language romantic comedy film directed by Kanmani. The story revolves around a young man named Shiva, played by Sundeep Kishan, who finds himself in a series of comedic situations after he accidentally switches his mobile phone with that of a young woman named Geetha, played by Surbhi. As they try to retrieve their phones, they become entangled in a web of misunderstandings and adventures, ultimately leading to love.
