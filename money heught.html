Money Heist (2017-2021)

Season 1
Episode 1

Description

"Money Heist" Season 1 Episode 1, titled "Efectuar lo acordado" (English: "Do as Planned"), introduces viewers to the mastermind behind the heist, known as "The Professor." He recruits a group of eight skilled individuals with unique talents to carry out his audacious plan: to infiltrate the Royal Mint of Spain and print billions of euros. The episode sets the stage for the high-stakes, intricate heist that will unfold throughout the series.

Episode 2

Description

In Season 1, Episode 2 of "Money Heist," titled "Imprudencias letales" (English: "Lethal Negligence"), tensions within the Royal Mint of Spain escalate as the robbers face unforeseen challenges. The episode delves deeper into the personal dynamics and backstories of the characters, revealing their motivations and vulnerabilities. Meanwhile, outside the Mint, the police begin to piece together clues about the identity and motives of the robbers. As the heist unfolds, the stakes continue to rise, setting the stage for even greater suspense and drama in the episodes to come.

Episode 3

Description

In Season 1, Episode 3 of "Money Heist," titled "Errar al disparar" (English: "Misfire"), tensions escalate both inside and outside the Royal Mint of Spain. The robbers are forced to adapt to unexpected developments while maintaining control over hostages and keeping their identities concealed. Meanwhile, the police intensify their efforts to apprehend the criminals, leading to a series of dramatic confrontations and close calls. As the heist continues to unfold, alliances are tested, secrets are revealed, and the stakes are raised even higher.

Episode 4

Description

In Season 1, Episode 4 of "Money Heist," titled "Caballo de Troya" (English: "Trojan Horse"), tensions reach a boiling point as the robbers face increasing pressure from both the police and their own internal conflicts. With the heist in full swing, the criminals must navigate a series of challenges and setbacks while trying to maintain control over the situation. Meanwhile, the police uncover new leads and strategies in their pursuit of the robbers, leading to a game of cat and mouse that intensifies with each passing moment. As the stakes continue to rise, alliances are tested and loyalties are questioned, setting the stage for an explosive climax.
