Bracking Bad (2008-2013)

Season 1
Episode 1

Description

"Breaking Bad" Season 1, Episode 1, titled "Pilot," introduces Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer. Faced with a terminal cancer diagnosis, Walter teams up with former student Jesse Pinkman to secure his family's financial future. The episode sets the stage for the moral dilemmas and consequences that follow Walter's decision to enter the world of drug production.
